# Chronon3d Roadmap

## Near-Term Engine Direction

The next architectural additions should be:

- **Render Graph & Passes**: Formalize the execution model with explicit passes.
- **Node & Asset Registries**: Centralize how resources are identified and managed.
- **Advanced Caching**: Geometry, text layout, and intermediate render result caching.
- **Precomposition Support**: Nested composition evaluation.
- **Video & Audio**: Robust video source handling and audio timeline metadata.
- **Color Pipeline**: Formal color space and sampling management.

## Vision

The goal is to maintain a high-performance, modular, and deterministic engine that can be easily embedded or used via CLI for automated motion graphics production.
