## What "Test 15 PASS" looks like

Read the file `transcripts/aggregate.md` (generated after the pilot completes) — if **the median of Q1 across ≥5 subjects is YES** AND **the median Q3 across ≥5 subjects is ≥ 10 min** AND **the page does not require any explanation** during administration, Test 15 PASSes.

If any condition fails, the one-pager goes back to draft — adjust the panel that confused subjects, not the harness.
